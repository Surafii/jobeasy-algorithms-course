The Classic Burger

  1. 1 pound ground lean(7% fat) beef
  2. Large egg
  3. 1/2 cup minced onion
  4. 1/4 cup fine dried bread crumbs
  5. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire
  6. 1 or 2 cloves garlic, piled and minced
  7. About 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. About 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  9. 4 Hamburger buns (4 in wide), split
  10. About 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions


  • Step 1

    • In a bowl, mix ground beef,egg,onion,bread crumbs,garlic,1/2 teaspoon salt.

  • Step 2

    • Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over a solid bed of hot coals.

  • Step 3

    • Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.

  • Step 4

    • Spread Mayonnaise and ketchup on burn bottoms, Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper.
